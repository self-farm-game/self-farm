# Product brief

**Self-Farm** is a tiny game about returning to yourself instead of spiraling.
The loop is one **care session**, not four chores:

> notice my state → choose a tiny quest → do it in real life → return and mark
> what changed → the tree grows → it's saved in the Farm Journal → runes unlock
> from repeated patterns.

- Audience: people stuck in noise/overstimulation who want a low-friction,
  non-clinical nudge toward small real actions.
- Home screen's one job: ask **«Як ти зараз?»** and lead into one small quest.
- Not medical, not therapy, not productivity, not a habit tracker, not a tamagotchi.
- Tone: warm, strange, playful, grounded — a post-apocalyptic-fairy-tale calm.

Hard rules live in `CLAUDE.md`; design tokens in `design-system.md`.
