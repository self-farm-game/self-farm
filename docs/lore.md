# Lore

The player lived in a **cave of shine** — bright, never dark, walls running with
ribbons of laughter, faces, other people's lives. A virus ("брейнрот") made the
shine brighter, the ribbons longer, the walls closer. One day they crawled out —
not heroically — into a plain, grey, honest **empty field**. The quiet wasn't
peace; it was loud, because now you could hear everything you brought with you.

A small square gnome climbs out of the weeds: **Бомбом**. Not a coach — warm,
grumpy, practical. He hands you one warm acorn:

> «Великого плану не буде. Нового життя з понеділка не буде. Ліс садити теж не
> будемо. Одне дерево. Один рух. Кажи, що там у тебе зараз.»

The first quest is to **name what's noisy** — not defeat it. A crooked sprout
appears. Не ліс, не спасіння — просто одна зелена штука, що росте там, де сухо.

The mechanics ARE the lore: **roots** = stability, **runes** = traces of repeated
returns, **quests** = tiny real actions matched to the state. The tree remembers
*returning*, not perfection, and never punishes a missed day.
